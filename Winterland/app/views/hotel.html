Room Types

Winterland Hotel features several room types to reach a wide range of needs

Lovers Nest

The lover's nest room is ideal for a romantic getaway, with features designed to make your stay as relaxing and special as possible.

  • King size bed
  • Large clawfoot tub that seats 3
  • A wide range of TV channels

Family Room

The family room is perfect for the family of 4, designed to not only relax the parents but to also entertain the kids.

  • Two bedrooms connected via central living room
  • Two queen size beds
  • Living room perfect for family activites
  • Two bathrooms, 1 per bedroom
  • Family freindly TV channels

Igloo

Our Igloo room is just that, an Igloo! Made from actual ice, and situated directly behind the main building, an igloo is perfect for the adventrourus single, or couple. (Spots Limited)

  • 15x15 feet floor plan
  • Fur lined queen sized bed (not real fur to any PITA activits)
  • Tv with premium channels
  • Bear Skin rug (again not real PITA)

All rooms come with several shared features:

  • Complimentry Meals (3 a day)
  • Optional itinerary including tours and special events
  • Ski Pass the local resort, good for duration of stay
